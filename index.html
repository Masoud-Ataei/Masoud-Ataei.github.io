Bio

My name is Masoud Ataei, and I am passionate about advancing safe control systems, particularly in the field of autonomous robotics. I am currently working on an innovative project to develop a safe control framework for autonomous vehicles. This project incorporates probabilistic safe control framework alongside a machine learning-based approach to safe navigation in uncertain environment.

I am also enthusiastic about sharing knowledge and collaborating on projects at the intersection of control theory, robotics, and machine learning.

If you’re interested in my work or potential collaborations, feel free to reach out!
